Is this really private?
Yes. Your files are opened and processed entirely inside your browser, on your own device — they are never uploaded to any server. The app itself downloads once from the web (and the tools it uses), then keeps working even with no connection. Because nothing leaves your device, no one — not even us — can see your files.
What Wype can and can't do
It removes: photo location (GPS), camera and date info, author and company names, document titles and keywords, reviewer names on tracked changes, and comments.

It can't (yet): recover or remove text someone "hid" under a black box in a PDF, and for Word files with heavy tracked-changes, the safest way to clear the deleted text itself is to also use Word's Review → Accept All Changes and Delete All Comments. Wype safely strips the identifying data and names; if a cleaned file ever won't open, your original is untouched — just re-save it from Word.
